Refund Policy

Refund requests are reviewed for verified billing issues and duplicate transactions.

Last updated: April 17, 2026

Eligibility

  • Duplicate payment for the same subscription period.
  • Technical billing mismatch verified by our payment logs.
  • Unauthorized transaction raised from registered account owner.

How to Raise a Request

  • Email support with registered email and transaction id.
  • Add payment date, amount, and a short issue summary.
  • Expected review window: up to 5 business days.
