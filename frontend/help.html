Frequently Asked Questions

Why is my timer auto-submitting at 00:00?

Auto-submit is part of exam realism. Once the timer ends, responses are locked and submitted automatically.

How do I continue an incomplete mock?

Open dashboard and use Continue Incomplete Mock. The system restores your saved progress and remaining time.

Why do weak-topic insights change between attempts?

Insights are recalculated from the latest section-wise accuracy and attempt patterns after each submitted mock.
