General Instructions

Please read the instructions carefully before starting the test:

  • The total duration of the examination is 60 minutes.
  • The clock will be set at the server. The countdown timer at the top right corner of the screen will display the remaining time available for you to complete the examination. When the timer reaches zero, the examination will end by itself.
  • The Question Palette displayed on the right side of the screen will show the status of each question using the following color codes:
Not Visited Not Answered Answered Marked for Review Answered & Marked for Review (will be evaluated)

Navigating to a Question

  • To answer a question, click on the question number in the Question Palette on the right side.
  • Click on "Save & Next" to save your answer and move to the next question.
  • Click on "Mark for Review" to mark the question for review and move to the next question.
  • Click on "Clear Response" to deselect the chosen answer.

Answering a Question

  • For MCQ type questions, choose one option by clicking on the radio button.
  • To change your answer, click on another option.
  • To save your answer, you MUST click on "Save & Next".
  • Use section tabs and palette colors to quickly identify unanswered and marked questions.

Marking Scheme

  • Correct Answer: +2 marks
  • Wrong Answer: -0.50 marks
  • Unanswered: 0 marks

Sections

  • Section 1: General Intelligence & Reasoning (25 Questions)
  • Section 2: General Awareness (25 Questions)
  • Section 3: Quantitative Aptitude (25 Questions)
  • Section 4: English Comprehension (25 Questions)

Submission Rules

  • When the timer reaches 00:00, the test auto-submits and editing is locked.
  • After manual submit, answers cannot be modified.
  • Section-wise score, speed, and weak-topic insights appear right after submission.
